Tel Aviv

Tel Aviv is the perfect place for a fun and easy getaway. You can enjoy 300 days of sunshine a year on miles of beautiful sandy beaches. It is a paradise for food lovers, famous for having some of the best hummus, falafel, and vegan food in the world. The city has a great energy where you can explore the ancient streets of Old Jaffa by day and a vibrant nightlife that stays open all night. It’s a friendly, modern city that is very easy to walk and explore.
